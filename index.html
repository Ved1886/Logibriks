GATE CHECK-IN YARD POSITIONING DOCK ALLOCATION OPERATIONS SYNC
STEP 01 // ENTRY SCAN

Terminal at the Gate

Logibrisk YOS automatically registers container IDs, license plates, and trailer security seals as trucks enter the terminal. Auto-validates e-way bills and opens gates in 1.5 seconds, eliminating queue lines.

STEP 02 // PARKING GRID

Terminal in the Yard

The AI dispatch engine calculates optimal slot assignments based on carrier type, destination zone, and cargo priority. Instantly routes shunters and yard jockeys dynamically, maximizing space utilization.

STEP 03 // CARGO HANDOFF

Terminal at the Dock

Real-time status updates sync loading dock gates with in-warehouse inventory. Shunter trucks are automatically scheduled for dock hookups when warehouse pickers finish prep, preventing idle dwell time.

STEP 04 // COMPLIANCE SYNC

Across Your Operations

Consolidated telemetry reconciles FASTag entries, toll transactions, customs status, and driver advance payments. Reconciled operational ledgers export directly to Tally ERP Prime, ensuring complete financial integrity.
